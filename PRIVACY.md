# Privacy Policy for ManageBac Task Completion Extension

**Last Updated: January 19, 2026**

## What We Collect

This extension collects basic usage information to help improve the product, including:

- Number of tasks marked as complete/incomplete
- General usage patterns (e.g., how often the extension is used)

## What We DON'T Collect

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit:

- Personal information (name, email, etc.)
- ManageBac login credentials
- Task content or descriptions
- Browsing history
- Any data from other websites

## How Data is Used

Usage information is collected solely to:

- Understand how the extension is being used
- Identify and fix bugs
- Improve features and functionality

## Data Storage

All task completion states are stored locally on your device using Chrome's local storage. This data never leaves your computer unless you explicitly sync it through Chrome's sync feature.

## Third-Party Access

We do not sell, trade, or share any data with third parties. Period.

## Children's Privacy

This extension is designed for students and complies with all applicable privacy laws. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
